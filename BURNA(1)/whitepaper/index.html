1. Introduction

The $BURNA Token is a fan-first Web3 project designed to align music culture with on-chain ownership, utility, and rewards. It turns cultural engagement into tangible value through tokenized access, governance, and community funding.

2. Vision & Mission

Vision

Become the definitive cultural currency of Afrobeat and digital expression—where fans and creators co-own what they build.

Mission

  • Empower fans via token-gated rewards & DAO voting
  • Support creators with fair smart-contract royalties
  • Fuse entertainment and DeFi (staking, LP incentives)
  • Deliver a mobile-first, borderless Web3 stack

Principles

  • Transparency (on-chain governance & treasury)
  • Community first (airdrops, grants, open tools)
  • Utility over hype (access, perks, economics)
  • Global access (education, low-friction onboarding)

3. The Problem

  • Fans don’t share in the value they create on Web2 platforms
  • Artists face middlemen and opaque revenue splits
  • No Afrobeat-native Web3 stack for NFTs/DAO/token economy
  • No formal community governance over culture & funding

4. The $BURNA Solution

Fan-Powered Ownership

DAO voting, proposal influence, token-gated access & rewards.

Creator Economy

Crowdfunded launches, on-chain royalties, and token-holder clubs.

NFT + DeFi

Utility NFTs (tickets, perks), staking APY and LP incentives.

Global Access

Mobile-first, low-gas routes, multilingual, and cross-chain path.
